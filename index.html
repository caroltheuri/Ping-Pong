Before we start playing,I want us to know what the game is about. This is a game that lets you know what numbers are divisible by what multiples.

Ping pong will count up to your number from 1 with the following exceptions:

Here are some of the rules:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 become "ping"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 become "pong"
  3. Numers divisible by both 3 and 5 become "ping-pong"
